Who is Kevin Najimi?

Kevin Najimi is a Solution Architect in the investment management space. He has 15 years of experience building quant research and algorithmic trading systems. He is an active member of the Chicago and New York algorithmic trading community. He studied at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee and the University of Chicago. He is also a CFA candidate.
