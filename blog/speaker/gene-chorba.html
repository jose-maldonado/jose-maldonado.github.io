Gene Chorba

Title: Implementing Encryption and Best Practices

Audience: All

Description

Python Development has featured commercial and government-grade encryption libraries from its earliest forms. Encryption libraries make it possible for organizations to protect data with the highest level of security. These days encrypted applications should be the rule not the exception. Through this speech I will walk you through the various encryption libraries available, the direction that encryption is going, how to implement them and best practices when using them. Example: How to integrate PiCrypto into your project, it's strengths and it's weaknesses.

Who is Gene Chorba?

While working as a Senior Developer Evangelist at Atlanta-based startup Ionic Security, Gene spends most weekends either speaking about security, or at a Hackathon. He has been written about in the NPR, AJC, NYT, Sports Illustrated, Huffington Post, Federal Times, and many other publications.
