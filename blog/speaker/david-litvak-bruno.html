Title: Fast Multi-Platform Prototyping: Quickly Create Apps and Content

Audience: Intermediate

Description

Prototyping and testing application ideas is very useful nowadays. We'll discover a few approaches to create apps across different platforms using the same content to test assumptions and deliver value to everyone. By using a 'headless' API-First CMS to consume our structured data, we're able to deliver quality content throughout a myriad of devices. We'll explore usage patterns that optimise our productivity and allow us to rapidly develop and test our ideas.
