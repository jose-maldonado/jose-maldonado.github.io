John Gill

Title: Climate, karma, pi and jupyter

Audience: All

Description

This talk will describe how the python community is supporting research everywhere that is helping our understanding of our world. Open sharing of knowledge, making research papers freely available, along with the data described in the papers. Extracting the signal from the noise.

Who is John Gill?

I am living in Bermuda and have been working on catastrophe modelling for 20 years. using free software throughout this time. I attended PyCaribbean in Santo Domningo last year and spoke about climate. I am also involved in raising awareness of free software, open data and the power of collaborative research here in Bermuda.
